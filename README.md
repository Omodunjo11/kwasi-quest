# Operation: Kwasi Comes to New York

**Live:** [kwasi-quest.vercel.app](https://kwasi-quest.vercel.app)

A personal, single-page interactive quest I built for my friend **Kwasi** before his first trip to New York — June 23–25, 2026.

## What it is

Not a product or portfolio piece. A retro RPG-style web experience disguised as a covert operation:

- **Cinematic intro** — "Operation: Kwasi Comes to New York" with a character sheet (stats, flags, vibes)
- **Quest map** — London pre-mission → landing zone → NYC chapters (Fish Cheeks, Milano's Bar, Grand Central, Brooklyn morning, Beacon, etc.)
- **XP system** — complete pre-trip tasks to unlock the next zone
- **The Treaty of New York** — binding clauses, signature required, no exceptions

Built as a surprise itinerary wrapper: the trip plan, inside jokes, and "Lapo leads" energy — delivered as a game instead of a Google Doc.

## Stack

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No framework, no build step. Deployed on Vercel.
